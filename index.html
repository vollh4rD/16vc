Our Philosophy

Independent Conviction, Collaborative Execution

+

We rely on independent conviction when making investment decisions, free from the need for internal consensus or external validation. However, once we invest, we collaborate closely with founders, providing strategic support, operational advice, and access to our global network to drive sustainable growth.

Efficient, Founder-Focused Investing

+

We respect the fast-paced nature of early-stage startups. Our streamlined diligence process ensures timely decisions, typically within two weeks. We offer flexible investment terms tailored to each company's unique needs, ensuring founders receive the right support at the right time.
